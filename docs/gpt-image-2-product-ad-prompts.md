# GPT Image 2 Product Ad Prompts and E-commerce Examples

This page collects GPT Image 2 prompts for product ads, e-commerce layouts, retail flyers, food campaigns, and promotional image compositions with clear commercial intent.

## Recommended GPT Image 2 Product Ad Prompt Patterns

- Describe the product surface clearly: product detail page, brand poster, supermarket flyer, food ad, or shopping app card.
- Include commerce-specific elements such as price, offer text, feature grid, packaging shot, usage scene, and CTA block.
- If you need a retail screenshot feel, mention the platform language directly such as Taobao, supermarket flyer, or store promo board.
